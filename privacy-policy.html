Privacy Policy

Last updated: 09 February 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when using Sleek JSON. Sleek JSON does not collect or store any personal information.

Introduction

Sleek JSON is a personal project created for formatting JSON efficiently. It is non-commercial and does not generate revenue through ads, tracking, or data collection.

Data Processing

All JSON processing occurs entirely within the user's web browser.

No data is transmitted to any servers or third-party services.

Cookies and Storage

Sleek JSON does not use cookies. The only data stored locally is the user's theme preference, which is saved in localStorage. This information remains on the user's device and is never transmitted to any servers.

Tracking and Analytics

Sleek JSON does not integrate with any tracking, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Hosting

Sleek JSON is hosted on Vercel. Users should review Vercel's privacy policy to understand how their data may be handled by Vercel. You can find Vercel's privacy policy here.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
